Modeling, identification and control of model jet engines for jet powered robotics

Giuseppe L'Erario et al., RAL 2020

  • Design a procedure of how to modeling, identify and control model jet engines.
  1. Use the sparse identification method SINDy to find out the governing jet engine model
    T¨=f(T,T˙,u)⇒T¨=a1+a+2T+a2T2+a3T˙+a4TT˙+a5T˙2+a6u+a7Tu+a8T˙u+a9u2
    After fixing the model shape, get:
    T¨=f(T,T˙)+g(T,T˙)v(u)
    where
    f(T,T˙)=KT+KTTT2+KDT˙+KDDT˙2+KTDTT˙+c,g(T,T˙)=BU+BT+BDT˙,v(u)=u+BUUu2
  2. Fixed the model structure, perform gray-box estimation using:
    1. Batch least squares
    2. Recursive extended Kalman Filter
      To identify p=(KT,KTT,KD,KDD,KTD,c,BU,BT,BD,BUU)⊤
  3. Control the model jet engine by applying and comparing feedback linearization and sliding mode control
